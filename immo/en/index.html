Real estate agency dashboard + AI

An interactive dashboard to manage an agency's leads and sales, with three machine-learning models that help decide where to focus energy and how to set prices.

Python scikit-learn pandas Plotly Streamlit
Open the interactive demo Opens in a new tab · on first load, the app may take a few seconds to wake up.
🎯

Lead scoring

A conversion probability for every lead: the agency knows who to call back first, instead of treating everyone the same.

Logistic regression · AUC ≈ 0.76
⏱️

Estimated time to sell

A number of days to sale, driven by how far the price per m² sits above the local market — a direct aid to setting the right price.

Linear regression · ± 6 days
📈

Revenue forecast

A projection of commissions over the coming months, with automatic recommendations on channels and response times.

Exponential smoothing (Holt)
Demo built with fictional but realistic data. For a real agency, I simply plug in its own data (CRM export / Excel) — the app works exactly the same.
